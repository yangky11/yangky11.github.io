Dr. Kaiyu Yang is a research scientist in the Meta Fundamental AI Research (FAIR) team. His research aims to build AI that can perform complex reasoning in mathematics. He has focused on using machine learning, especially large language models, to reason and prove theorems in formal proof assistants such as Coq and Lean. Before joining FAIR, he was a postdoctoral scholar at Caltech. He received a Ph.D. in computer science from Princeton University and bachelor's degrees in computer science and mathematics from Tsinghua University.
